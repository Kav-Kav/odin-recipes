Jasmine Rice

Description

This is an excellent authentic Mexican rice recipe (not to be confused with Spanish rice) that I make as a side dish with all of my Mexican dishes. The key is cooking the rice properly and using good quality chicken broth or stock.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 1 ½ cups chicken broth
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 fresh jalapeno pepper, chopped

Steps

  1. In a medium sauce pan, cook rice in oil over medium heat for about 3 minutes.
  2. Pour in chicken broth, and bring to a boil. Stir in onion, green pepper, jalapeno, and diced tomato.
  3. Season with bouillon cube, salt and pepper, cumin, cilantro, and garlic.
  4. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to low.
  5. Cook for 20 minutes.
